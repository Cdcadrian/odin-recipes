Hot Slaw

Description:

My goto recipe to get alot of fiber and vegetables,

without sacrificing on flavor.

Ingredients

  • Bag of mixed and chopped Cabbage and carrots
  • Salt
  • Gochujuang paste
  • Soy Sauce

Steps:

  1. Get a non-stick pan, heat to very high
  2. Add butter, wait for it to fully melt and heat to just before browning
  3. Add egg, then quickly baste the egg with the butter, until top is cooked
  4. Wait for a semi-dark crispy edge to form, remove from pan onto plate with paper towel
