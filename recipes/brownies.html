Brownies

Description:

Here is a recipe for some gooey brownies that

always come out looking very nice, have some

nice saltiness to them and overall are very tasty.

Ingredients:

  • 62 grams all-purpose flour
  • 50 grams cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 Diamond Crystal salt, or 3/4 Morton kosher salt
  • 1 stick butter
  • 113 grams chocolate
  • 250 grams granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 113 grams chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Grease a 8 inch baking tray, line with greased parchment paper
  2. whisk flour, cocoa powder, and salt until combined
  3. combine butter and chocolate in a medium bowl, set over a sauce pan of barely simmering water, heat until chocolate is melted and smooth
  4. whisk in sugar, then add 3 eggs one at a time, whisking vigorously
  5. whisk in vanilla extract, then whisk in dry ingredients, stir in chocolate chips
  6. bake until set across top and tester comes with a few moist crumbs attached, 27-30 minutes
  7. Let cool for 30 minutes, or overnight, cut into pieces, enjoy.
